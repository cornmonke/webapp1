How to use this site

This site is a simple website which displays a pixelated image of my study room where I spent most of my time. Drag the mouse around and click on different items to learn more about them and what they mean to me. Enjoy!

What is this site?

This is my first (solo) website :D. It was made after I found interest in attending a hackathon in December 2025 (I totally got carried by my teammate). Being project 1, this website is focused on styling, css and html templates without delving to much into flask backend. During this project, I learned how to manipulate div elements, fonts, background images, template inheritance and how to customize the appearance of a webpage.
